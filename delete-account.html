Delete Your Account

ToDo Notification List

You can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to the address below with the subject line "Delete My Account". We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 days.

Email Us to Delete Account
⚠️ This action is permanent and irreversible. All your data will be deleted and cannot be recovered.

Data that will be permanently deleted

  • Your Firebase account and authentication credentials
  • All recurring notifications you have created
  • Your device push notification tokens (FCM / APNs)
  • Your subscription and purchase verification records
  • Any feedback messages you have submitted

Data that is retained

  • None — all data associated with your account is fully deleted

Retention period

All data is deleted immediately upon processing your request. No backups or archives of your personal data are retained after deletion.

Active subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play or App Store subscription. To avoid future charges, please cancel your subscription separately through:

  • Google Play → Subscriptions
  • App Store → Subscriptions
