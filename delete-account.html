No Linked Account

Your data is tied to your device and cannot be verified from a web page.

Because you never linked a Google or Apple account to the app, your account exists only on your device. To delete your data, use one of the options below:

Option 1 — Delete from the app

Open the app on your iPhone → go to Settings → tap Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google to link your account first, then return here.

Option 2 — Delete from your device

Simply delete the app from your iPhone. Since your account was never linked to a Google or Apple identity, uninstalling the app removes all local data. Any anonymous cloud data will be automatically purged by our system within 30 days of inactivity.

Option 3 — Email us

If you need manual deletion sooner, email us and we will process your request within 7 days.
