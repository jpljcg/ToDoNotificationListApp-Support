# Privacy Policy – ToDo Notification List

Effective date: 2026-02-19

ToDo Notification List ("the app") helps users manage one-off and recurring reminders.

## 1. Data We Process
Depending on features you use, the app may process:
- Account identifiers needed for cloud sync
- Reminder/task data you create in the app
- Subscription and purchase status information (via Apple/Google billing)
- Diagnostic/crash data to improve app stability

## 2. How We Use Data
We use data to:
- Provide core app functionality (reminders, sync, restore purchases)
- Maintain subscription access
- Improve reliability and fix issues

## 3. Notifications
The app requests notification permission to deliver reminders you configure.

## 4. Purchases
In-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store / Google Play.
We do not receive your full payment card details.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data.
Data may be processed by service providers required to operate the app (e.g., cloud/auth/crash reporting services).

## 6. Data Retention
Data is retained only as long as needed to provide app functionality, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

## 7. Your Choices
You can:
- Disable notifications in your device settings
- Uninstall the app
- Contact support to request help with account-related data requests

## 8. Children
The app is not directed to children under 13.
